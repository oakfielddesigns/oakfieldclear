Deep Pour

Deep Pour is an epoxy resin specially formulated for river tables, serving boards, bar and bench tops, anything that requires a large deep pour of epoxy. This resin can be poured to depths of up to 40mm in one go. Superb clarity, excellent UV resistance, low bubble and predictable curing behaviour.

Clarity Cast

Clarity Cast is an epoxy resin that offers superior clarity for pieces that require a shallower pour. Suitable for casting small items, coasters, lamps and other smaller pieces. This resin can be poured to depths of up to 20mm in one go. Superb clarity, excellent UV resistance, low bubble and predictable curing behaviour.

Ultimate Glass

Ultimate Glass is an epoxy resin designed for small pours a few mm thick, such as pens or jewellery, resin art such as beach waves, or for extremely clear, smooth and shiny surface coatings. As the name suggests, the finish and clarity are like clear glass. The cycloaliphatic curing agent offers superior UV protection.
